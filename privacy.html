Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 25, 2026

1. Overview

InstaForge Publisher ("the Application") is a content management platform that enables creators to produce and publish original video content to social media platforms. This privacy policy explains how the Application handles data.

2. Data Collection

InstaForge Publisher does not collect, store, or process any personal data from end users or viewers. The Application is used solely by its operator (the content creator) to publish original content to social media platforms.

The Application does not collect analytics, tracking data, or personal information from any third party.

3. Authentication & API Access

The Application uses OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with social media platforms including TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, and YouTube. This authentication allows the Application to publish content on behalf of the operator.

OAuth tokens are stored securely on the operator's own private infrastructure. No tokens, credentials, or authentication data are shared with third parties or stored on external servers.

4. TikTok Integration

When connected to TikTok, the Application uses the TikTok Content Posting API to publish video content. The Application:

• Only publishes content explicitly approved by the operator
• Does not access or store TikTok user data beyond the operator's own account
• Does not collect data from TikTok viewers or followers
• Does not use TikTok data for advertising or analytics beyond the operator's own content performance

5. Data Sharing

InstaForge Publisher does not sell, share, or distribute any data to third parties. The only data transmitted externally is the content (videos, captions, metadata) published to connected social media platforms via their official APIs.

6. Data Storage & Security

All application data, including configuration, content queue, and authentication tokens, is stored locally on the operator's own private servers. Data is encrypted in transit using HTTPS/TLS. No data is stored in third-party cloud services.

7. Data Retention

Published content metadata is retained in the Application's local database for content management purposes. The operator may delete this data at any time. Social media platforms may retain published content according to their own data retention policies.

8. Cookies & Tracking

This website and the Application do not use cookies, web beacons, or any tracking technologies.

9. Children's Privacy

This Application is not directed at children under 13. The operator does not knowingly publish content targeting children or collect data from minors.

10. Your Rights

Since the Application does not collect personal data from end users, there is no personal data to access, correct, or delete. If you believe your data has been inadvertently processed, please contact us.

11. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated to reflect changes in the Application's functionality or applicable regulations. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated revision date.

12. Contact

For questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact us at: contact@instaforge.app
