Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 6, 2026

1. Overview

InstaForge Publisher is a personal content management tool. This privacy policy explains how the application handles data.

2. Data Collection

InstaForge Publisher does not collect, store, or process any personal data from end users. The application is used solely by its operator to publish original content to social media platforms.

3. Authentication

The application uses OAuth 2.0 to authenticate with social media platforms (TikTok, Instagram, Facebook, YouTube). Authentication tokens are stored locally and securely on the operator's own infrastructure. No tokens or credentials are shared with third parties.

4. Data Sharing

InstaForge Publisher does not share any data with third parties beyond what is necessary to publish content to the connected social media platforms via their official APIs.

5. Data Storage

All data is stored locally on the operator's own servers. No data is stored in third-party cloud services beyond what the connected social media platforms retain as part of their normal operations.

6. Cookies

This application does not use cookies or tracking technologies.

7. Children's Privacy

This application is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

9. Contact

For questions about this privacy policy, please contact via the associated social media channels.
